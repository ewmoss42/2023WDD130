Family Home Evening

Family home evening is a time for patience and mutual respect. James E. Faust

We, as members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, think it's important to spend at least one evening a week to the family.

family gathering

Family Home Evening is often held on Monday nights, but alternative times can work if they better suit your family's needs.

How to get started

The following advice can be helpful if you are new to Family Home Evening or simply need a little help getting organized. Review the broad plan. Simply in the blanks or make a few minor adjustments to suit the demands of your household.
